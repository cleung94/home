Quick Summary

Moka & Co is a strong pick if you want a calm place to sit down, talk, study, or spend a little extra time with coffee and dessert. The environment is one of its strongest qualities, and the parking situation is easier than a lot of busy café spots in the area.

The drinks and desserts are good overall, with a few personal preference notes around sweetness and texture. Service is efficient, especially when it is busy, but it feels less guided if you are hoping for suggestions or a warmer recommendation experience.

How This Review Is Scored

I score each category out of 10, then average the five category scores to create the final restaurant rating.

(Ambience + Food Quality + Service Quality + Menu + Parking) ÷ 5 = Final Score

Ambience

8.0 / 10
  • Calm environment where people can talk without talking over each other
  • A lot of tables for studying, hanging out, or playing board games
  • Good spot for a longer sit-down visit

The biggest drawback is the outlet situation. There are not many plug options, so bringing a power strip is actually a smart move if you plan to stay and work.

Food Quality

7.5 / 10
  • Biscoff latte had a nice flavor profile
  • Tiramisu cheesecake was good and felt well made
  • Overall quality was solid for both the drink and dessert

The Biscoff latte can land sweeter and milkier toward the end, so less sweetness is probably the better order if you are sensitive to sugar. The cheesecake was enjoyable, though the crust leaned more crumbly than chewy, which is more of a personal preference point for me.

Service Quality

7.0 / 10
  • Efficient during busy periods
  • Gets customers through quickly
  • Service feels functional and direct

The staff seems busy and focused, but the experience is not especially welcoming. They do not really guide you through the menu or offer suggestions strongly, so it feels more like a place where you should already know what you want after reading the options yourself.

Menu

8.0 / 10
  • Diverse selection
  • Has the basics you would want from a coffee house
  • Includes baked goods and desserts alongside drinks

The menu does a good job covering what most people would expect from a café visit while still feeling broad enough to give you options beyond a single standard coffee order.

Parking

9.0 / 10
  • Private lot with a good amount of spaces
  • Additional parking available farther in the back
  • Located off a main road and the parking feels fairly safe

The only minor tradeoff is that overflow parking can turn into a short three-minute walk, but that is still better than fighting for a single tight storefront spot.

Final Verdict

Moka & Co is a café I would recommend more for atmosphere and hangout value than for ultra-personalized service. If you want a quieter place to sit down with a drink, dessert, and a little more room to stay awhile, it does a lot of things well.

It is especially good for study sessions, casual meetups, and relaxed coffee runs. Just order your drink a little less sweet if that matters to you, and do not count on a lot of outlet access.
