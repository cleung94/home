Quick Summary

Moka & Co is a strong pick if you want a calm place to sit down, talk, study, or spend a little extra time with coffee and dessert. The environment is one of its strongest qualities, and the parking situation is easier than a lot of busy café spots in the area.

The drinks and desserts are good overall, with a few personal preference notes around sweetness and texture. Service is efficient, especially when it is busy, but it feels less guided if you are hoping for suggestions or a warmer recommendation experience.

Ambience

8.0 / 10
Calm environment where people can talk without talking over each other
A lot of tables for studying, hanging out, or playing board games
Not many plug outlets — bring a power strip if you plan to stay

This is one of the strongest parts of the café for me. It is easy to settle in here for a longer visit without the space feeling too loud or crowded.

Food Quality

7.5 / 10

Desserts felt well made overall
The drink leaned too milky and sweet toward the end
Crust was crumbly instead of chewy (preference)

The drinks and desserts are good overall, but a few small preference points kept this from being higher for me.

Service Quality

7.0 / 10

Menu

8.0 / 10

This is a well-rounded menu and one of the reasons the place feels easy to revisit.

Parking

9.0 / 10
Private lot with plenty of spaces
Safe and easy location
2–3 minute walk if parking fills up

Parking is still one of the better parts of the experience here, even with the slight extra walk when it gets busier.

Final Verdict

Moka & Co is a café I would recommend more for atmosphere and hangout value than for ultra-personalized service. If you want a quieter place to sit down with a drink, dessert, and a little more room to stay awhile, it does a lot of things well.

It is especially good for study sessions, casual meetups, and relaxed coffee runs. Just order your drink a little less sweet if that matters to you, and do not count on a lot of outlet access.
