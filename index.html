Hello! I am Christopher

About Me

I am a technology and operations professional with experience supporting large-scale enterprise physical security systems and data-driven quality assurance initiatives. I specialize in analyzing system performance, developing operational standards, and building reporting tools that improve efficiency and visibility across complex environments.

In my current role, I collaborate with cross-functional teams to monitor system health, audit device deployments, and develop automation and reporting processes using tools such as Power BI, Tableau, Excel, and Python. My work has helped streamline workflows, improve system compliance, and provide actionable insights for operational decision-making.

Alongside my technical background, I have also pursued clinical experience as a Certified Nursing Assistant, where I provide direct patient care and support residents with daily living activities. This experience has strengthened my communication, situational awareness, and commitment to work that has a meaningful impact on others.

I am passionate about continuous learning and enjoy exploring the intersection of technology, operations, and real-world problem solving.
