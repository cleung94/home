Overview

Designed to support smoother handoffs and better shift organization

This project was created to improve the way CNA shift information is captured and carried throughout the workday. During busy shifts, caregivers often need to track multiple responsibilities at once, including patient notes, toileting, vitals, supplies, and shift updates. I designed this tool to create a more structured and visually intuitive way to manage that information.

The layout mirrors the natural flow of a CNA shift, making it easier to quickly record important details, reference them during rounds, and communicate clearly during handoff. After sharing it with coworkers, it received positive feedback for its usefulness and clarity.

Case Study

Problem, goal, and solution

Problem

CNA shifts can involve managing multiple patients, frequent task switching, and quick communication during report. Important details can become scattered across memory, rough notes, or separate documentation habits.

Goal

Create a structured, easy-to-scan shift helper that supports patient care tracking, note-taking, inventory checks, and smoother handoff communication.

Solution

Designed a color-coded shift organizer with dedicated sections for assignments, handoff notes, vitals, supply inventory, and shift notes to reduce mental load and improve usability during busy rounds.

Highlights

What this tool helps with

Shift Handoff Clarity

Centralizes report notes and key details so information is easier to carry into the next shift.

Vitals Tracking

Provides dedicated spaces for blood pressure, temperature, pulse, and oxygen so documentation can be captured quickly.

Inventory Monitoring

Includes supply tracking for wipes, briefs, pads, gloves, and other essentials to help staff identify shortages early.

Better Organization

Uses visual grouping and check-box style inputs to make information easier to scan at a glance during fast-paced shifts.
